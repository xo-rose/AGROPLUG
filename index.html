The AgroPlug Blueprint

Why we do things differently around here.

✨ How is AgroPlug different from big agro platforms?

Those platforms deal with massive commercial supply chains and long-distance shipping. AgroPlug is for the local hustle. We connect you directly to nearby farms so you can score fresh harvests on the same day without corporate middle-men or long wait times.

💸 Are there any hidden transaction fees?

Nope. Zero fees. AgroPlug is completely free to use. We don't trap your money in online digital wallets or take a percentage cut from the farmer's profit.

🔌 Why do transactions happen over WhatsApp?

Because everyone already uses WhatsApp! Instead of making you learn a complicated, clunky checkout system, hitting "Claim" securely connects buyer and seller on WhatsApp so you can negotiate delivery and payment instantly on your own terms.
